字符串分割

    <#list "a|b|c|d|e"?split("|") as item >
  • ${item}

字符串转日期

    <#assign a="2018/12/12"?date("yyyy/MM/dd") /> <#assign b="2018-12-12 12:12:12"?datetime("yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss") /> <#assign c="12:12:12"?time("HH:mm:ss") />
  • ${a}
  • ${b}
  • ${c}

数字格式化

    <#assign a=3.1415926?string("0.###") />
  • ${a}

数字四舍五入

    <#assign a=3.1415926?round />
  • ${a}

List分组

    <#assign myList=[1,2,3,4,11,22,33,44,111,222,333,444] />
  • ${myList?chunk(4)?size}
    • <#list myList?chunk(4)?last as item >
  • ${item}

其他内建函数

    <#assign a="hello" />
  • ${a?is_number?string('true','false')}
  • ${a?has_content?string('true','false')}
